The Rules!!

  • It's a game of atleast 3 and atmost 7 players.
  • There's a deck of cards with every number between 3 and 35
  • Nine of these cards are randomly removed from the game and the rest are shuffled and are played with.
  • Each of the players gets a fixed number of tokens at the start of the game.
  • In each turn, a card is turned over from the deck of cards.
  • The active player can either pass the card over, along with one of their tokens to the next player or take the card with all the tokens on it.
  • If you have no tokens left, you must take the card
  • Once all the cards have been taken, the game ends.
  • Each players score is the sum of the numbers on their cards minus one point for each token with them.
    ie, Score = sum(Cards) - Number of tokens
  • The player with least points wins the game
  • In other terms, cards are bad and tokens are good.
  • Annnd the last one... If you've got a stack of consecutive cards, only the lowest card of the stack counts. That is,
